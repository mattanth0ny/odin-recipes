Chicken Parmesan

This is a wonderful recipe for Chicken Parmesan

This dish consists of a chicken cutlet that has been breaded and fried, with marinara and mozarella cheese melted atop of it.

It can be served with pasta or eaten by itself. Serve atop a pouring of creamy polenta for a real winter treat!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Cutlet
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Eggs
  • Oil for frying
  • Mozarella cheese
  • Optional:Parmesan for sprinkling

Steps

  1. Season chicken
  2. Dip in egg mixture and then bread crumbs
  3. Fry
